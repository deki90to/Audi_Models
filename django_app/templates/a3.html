The Audi A3 is a small family or subcompact executive car manufactured and marketed since the 1990s[1][2] by the Audi subdivision of the Volkswagen Group, now in its 3rd generation. The first two generations of the Audi A3 were based on the Volkswagen Group A platform, which they share with the Audi TT, Volkswagen Golf, Volkswagen Caddy, and Volkswagen Touran, as well as SEAT León, SEAT Toledo, and Škoda Octavia.
